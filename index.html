Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, SLDS, Angular, C#, ASP.Net WebAPI, APS.Net MVC, SQL Server, Go, Microservices

This project was initiated to reduce the operational burden of alert resolution on service owners, enabling them to prioritize feature development and automation initiatives. It introduced a robust control plane for alert management, providing both semi-automated and fully automated resolution capabilities.

The semi-automated workflows guide users through the resolution process with minimal effort, requiring only basic configuration inputs. The fully automated workflows extract critical details from alerts and execute predefined Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).

Key features of this control plane includes:

  • Enhanced Security: Leveraging the Principles of Least Privilege to minimize the risk of production resource mishandling.
  • Comprehensive Audit and Tracking: Ensuring transparency and accountability by maintaining a detailed log of user actions.
  • Proactive Monitoring: Facilitating metric collection for early detection and resolution of potential issues. This solution streamlines alert management while ensuring operational safety, transparency, and efficiency.
